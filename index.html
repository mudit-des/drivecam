Troubleshooting

Make sure you have the right MicroSD card before setup.

DriveCam writes footage non-stop, every time you drive. A regular phone card wears out quietly — you won't know until you need the recording and it isn't there. Use an endurance-rated card. Class 10 / U3 / V30. Built for continuous writes. Built to last. Storage up to 512GB supported.

Recommended

Samsung PRO Endurance microSD, SanDisk High Endurance microSD, or Lexar Professional 1066x microSD.
