Get the right MicroSD card before you start.

DriveCam records continuously, so use an endurance-rated card built for constant writing. Class 10, U3, V30. Supports up to 512GB.

Recommended

Samsung PRO Endurance microSD, SanDisk High Endurance microSD, or Lexar Professional 1066x microSD.
